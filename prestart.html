Overview

Before initiating the reactor startup sequence, it is imperative to complete the Pre-Startup Checklist. The checklist ensures all critical systems are within operational parameters and prevents damage or hazardous events during startup.

Location: The checklist is located on the table beside the control computer. Operators must manually confirm each step is completed—order of completion is flexible.

Recommendation: Review the Control Room Overview for familiarity with systems before beginning.

Pre-Startup Checklist

1. Condenser Coolant Pumps

  • Condenser Coolant Pump A1 – Enabled
  • Condenser Coolant Pump A2 – Enabled
  • A1 Speed – 2
  • A2 Speed – 2

2. Turbine Settings

  • Turbine A-B Bleed Valve – Open
  • Turbine A Bypass Valve – Enabled
  • Turbine A Bypass – 100% (adjust knob)

3. Deaerator Settings

  • A-B Bleed Valve – Closed
  • Relief Valve – 50%
  • Steam Inlet Valve – 25%

4. Recirculation Pumps

  • Pump A1 Speed – 2
  • Pump A2 Speed – 2

5. Reactor Isolation Valves

  • Inlet 1 & 2 – Open
  • Outlet 1 & 2 – Open

6. ECCS Injection Tanks

  • Pressure – ≥ 1500 PSI (toggle Nitrogen valve to fill)

7. Hotwell Control

  • Automatic Level – 35 (numpad + grey button)
  • Limiter Valve – Enabled

8. Electrical Panel

  • Breaker 18M-G1 – On
  • Breaker 64B-M – On (key in orange box near Control Room entrance)
  • Automatic G1 Offload – Disabled
  • G1<64A Offload System – Primed

9. Feed Limiter Valve

  • Valve – Enabled (next to feedwater pumps)
